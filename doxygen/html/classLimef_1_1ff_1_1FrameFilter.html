pure virtual

Implementation of the framefilter action.

Uses Limef::ff:FrameFilter::pass to pass frames downstream.

The framefilter ensures that the frame is not modified. If it needs a modified frame it has to take an internal copy of the frame first.

Implemented in Limef::ff::DecodedToTensorFrameFilter, Limef::ff::TensorToDecodedFrameFilter, Limef::ff::AudioTranscodeFrameFilter, Limef::ff::CodecCacheFrameFilter, Limef::ff::CodecMessageFrameFilter, Limef::ff::ComposeFrameFilter, Limef::ff::DecodingFrameFilter, Limef::ff::DumpFrameFilter, Limef::ff::DumpPacketFrameFilter, Limef::ff::EmptyFrameFilter, Limef::ff::EncodeCheckFrameFilter, Limef::ff::EncodingFrameFilter, Limef::ff::FrameFifoFrameFilter< T >, Limef::ff::FrameFifoFrameFilter< Limef::frame::DecodedFrame >, Limef::ff::FrameFifoFrameFilter< Limef::frame::TensorFrame >, Limef::ff::GateFrameFilter, Limef::ff::InfoFrameFilter, Limef::ff::MP4ProbeFrameFilter, Limef::ff::MP4PartitionFrameFilter, Limef::ff::MuxerFrameFilter, Limef::ff::RTPMuxerFrameFilter, Limef::ff::ShmemServerFrameFilter, Limef::ff::SplitFrameFilter, Limef::ff::StreamGateFrameFilter, Limef::ff::SwitchFrameFilter, Limef::ff::SwScaleFrameFilter, and Limef::ff::UploadGPUFrameFilter.
